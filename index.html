ROLE OF REMOTE SENSING FOR
AGRICULTURE AND RURAL DEVELOPEMENT

VENUE: TRP-MAIN HALL
DATE: 13th July 2016

A Guest Lecture was delivered in the topic Role
of remote sensing for agriculture and rural
developement on 13th July 2016 in IEEE TIAR
international conference. The Speker for the
Guest Lecture is S. VidhyaLakshmi who is head
of civil department in S.A Engineering College

The audience of this guest lecture are 2nd, 3rd
and 4th year of IT department

The guest lecture is about the advancements that can be acheived by the agriculture with the help of remote sensing and how it is currently linked with the agriculture
